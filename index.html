<About Me>

Having been known as a 'gadgetologist' most of my life, I'm a web developer specializing in JAVASCRIPT and its myriad libraries and companion technologies to create. Having coded my first video game in BASIC as a boy, I then spent my life as an opera singer - always with my eye on tech and my fingers on a keyboard.

A Photographer who knows Photoshop and an artist who knows how to find answers to complex problems with creativity, I have an uncanny ability to communicate with clients about the requirements of their applications and business functionality using a database, server and UI. Working WITH people is a developed skill, so group interactions are made to move more smoothly and projects are made to come together with speed in a better work environment.

A recent graduate of Rutgers University CS bootcamp, I'm versed in the many technologies used today in complex front and back-end systems such as Javascript, React, node.js, HTML, CSS, MYSQL, MONGODB, express, handlebars, jquery, sequelize, among others.
